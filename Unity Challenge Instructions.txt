1. Open Unity Challenge Folder in the Unity Hub
2. Press the play button located above the scene viewer, starting the Trash Dash game

Test 5 lives:
1. Select Run menu button with mouse and game should start
2. Use the arrows keys to move left and right, as well as jump and slide.
3. If you collide with an obstacle you will lose a life.
4. When you hit 5, the game ends. 

To see Mission list:
1. There's a menu button located at the bottom of the game screen. Whether it's on the main menu or game over screen.
2. Click to open with mouse. 
3. To scroll up and down, click and hold mouse and move up or down.
